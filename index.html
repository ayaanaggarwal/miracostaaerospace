What we work on

Three active teams, one shared mission: build things that fly.

🚀

Rocketry

In partnership with the Hermosa Beach Rocketry Club, we design, build, and launch high-power rockets at Lucerne Dry Lake. Members can pursue NAR certification.

Visit HBRC page →
🛸

Drone & UAV

From FPV racing quads to autonomous fixed-wing platforms, our drone team explores aerial robotics, computer vision, and autonomous navigation systems.

See projects →
🔬

Research & Science

Payload experiments, atmospheric data collection, and STEM outreach. Our research team documents every flight and presents findings at regional science fairs.

See projects →
Our mission

Engineering meets adventure at 10,000 feet

We're not just a club — we're a launchpad. Every student who joins MCAC leaves with real engineering experience, hands-on build skills, and a network of mentors from the aerospace industry.

About the club →
🛠️

Build real things

No simulations. We build rockets, drones, and payloads from scratch using real engineering tools.

📐

Learn by doing

CAD, OpenRocket, flight software, electronics — skills you'll use in college and beyond.

🌍

Give back

We run intro rocketry workshops for younger students and local scout troops throughout the year.

🏆

Compete

We enter regional and national competitions including TARC, AeroPac, and science olympiad events.
