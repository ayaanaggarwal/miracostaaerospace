Autonomous Waypoint Drone

Building a fixed-wing UAV capable of flying a pre-programmed GPS waypoint route using ArduPilot and a Pixhawk flight controller. Target range: 2km.

ArduPilot Pixhawk Fixed-wing GPS

Research

Atmospheric Payload — CanSat

A soda-can-sized payload measuring temperature, pressure, humidity, and UV index during rocket flights. Data logged to SD card and transmitted live via LoRa radio.

CanSat Arduino LoRa Sensors

Rocketry

L1 Certification Rocket — "Mustang"

Our club's first successful L1 certification flight. 3" diameter phenolic airframe, H238 motor. Successfully flown and recovered at Lucerne Dry Lake, November 2024.

H-motor Phenolic NAR L1 Recovered
